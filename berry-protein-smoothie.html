Berry Protein Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 30g
  • Calories: 320
  • Carbohydrates: 28g
  • Fat: 6g

Instructions

  1. Add berries, milk, yogurt and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add honey if desired.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Supports muscle recovery
  • High in protein
  • Great post-workout smoothie
