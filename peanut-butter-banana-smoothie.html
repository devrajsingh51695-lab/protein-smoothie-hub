Peanut Butter Banana Protein Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 38g
  • Calories: 450
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fat: 18g

Instructions

  1. Add banana, peanut butter, milk and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve immediately.

Benefits

  • High-calorie smoothie for muscle gain
  • Excellent source of protein
  • Provides healthy fats
  • Great breakfast or post-workout drink
